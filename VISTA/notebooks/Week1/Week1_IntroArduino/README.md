## ğŸ§  Microcontroller vs. Microprocessor

| Feature               | Microcontroller (Arduino) | Microprocessor (Computer) |
|----------------------|---------------------------|----------------------------|
| Clock Speed          | ~16 MHz                   | ~2+ GHz                   |
| RAM                  | ~2 KB                     | ~8+ GB                    |
